#### 一些总结

##### 区别poke/jab/prod/thrust

- poke 尖长细物扎、戳 
- jab 短快准，以伤害为出发点 
- prod most gentle and less abrupt; try to guide sb in certain direction
- thrust a force generated by an engineen
